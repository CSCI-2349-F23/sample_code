 The brown false shieldback (Aroegas fuscus) is a species of katydid that is only known from two localities; in Mpumalanga and in Limpopo provinces, South Africa. It only occurs at elevations above 1,200 m in the mesic highveld grasslands. This article about a member of the insect family Tettigoniidae is a stub. You can help Wikipedia by expanding it.
